📘 What is SIP?

What is SIP?

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) allows you to invest small amounts regularly in mutual funds.

Benefits of SIP

SIP helps build long-term wealth through disciplined investing and compounding growth.

Power of Compounding

Your investment earns returns, and those returns generate additional returns over time.

Why Start Early?

Starting SIP early gives more time for wealth multiplication and reduces financial stress.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SIP?

SIP is a method of investing regularly in mutual funds.

Is SIP safe?

SIP reduces market timing risk through disciplined investing.

Can I stop SIP anytime?

Yes, most SIP investments can be stopped or modified anytime.

What return can SIP generate?

Returns depend on market performance and fund type.
